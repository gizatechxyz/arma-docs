---

ARMA's fee structure is designed to be transparent and aligned with user interests:

## Performance Fee
- 10% of generated yield
- Charged only on positive returns
- Calculated and collected monthly

## Gas Costs
- Paid from your smart account
- Optimized for efficiency
- Factored into yield calculations

No other hidden fees or charges apply.
