title: "Overview"
---

ARMA is an autonomous yield optimization agent designed to maximize returns on stablecoin deposits across Base and Mode Network's leading lending protocols. ARMA continuously monitors and evaluates opportunities across protocols like AAVE, Morpho, Compound, Moonwell and more. ARMA automatically reallocates funds to the highest-yielding pools, taking into account APR, protocol rewards and ecosystem rewards while minimizing transaction costs and maintaining robust security and full user control.
