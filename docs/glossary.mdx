## Key Terms

**APY**: Annual Percentage Yield - The effective annual rate of return taking into account compound interest.

**TVL**: Total Value Locked - The total amount of assets deposited in a protocol.

**Smart Account**: A smart contract wallet that holds your funds and executes transactions.

**Session Key**: A limited authorization mechanism that allows the agent to perform specific actions.

**Gas Fees**: Transaction costs paid to the network for executing operations.

**Rebalancing**: The process of moving funds between protocols to optimize returns.
