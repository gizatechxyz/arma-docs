## Security Architecture

ARMA implements multiple layers of security:

1. **Self-Custodial Design**
   - Your funds remain in your control
   - Smart contract wallet with your ownership
   - Limited permissions for the agent

2. **Session Keys**
   - Time-limited authorizations
   - Restricted operation scope
   - Revocable at any time

3. **Smart Contract Safety**
   - Audited contracts
   - Emergency pause functionality
   - Withdrawal guarantees

## Risk Management

- Protocol diversification
- Position size limits
- Continuous monitoring
- Automatic circuit breakers
